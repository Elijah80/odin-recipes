Beef Stroganoff

Description:

Beef Stroganoff is a classic comfort dish that features tender strips of beef, savory mushrooms, and a creamy sauce. This recipe combines these elements to create a flavorful and satisfying meal. Serve it over egg noodles or rice for a hearty dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) beef sirloin, thinly sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces (225g) mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • 8 ounces (225g) egg noodles or rice, cooked

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the beef strips and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, melt the butter. Saut√© the chopped onion until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and sliced mushrooms. Cook until mushrooms are browned and any released liquid evaporates.
  4. Sprinkle flour over mushrooms and stir to combine. Gradually pour in beef broth, stirring constantly. Simmer until the sauce thickens.
  5. Stir in Dijon mustard and Worcestershire sauce. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Reduce heat and return cooked beef to the skillet. Heat through, then remove from heat.
  7. Gently fold in sour cream, avoiding boiling.
  8. Serve Stroganoff over cooked egg noodles or rice. Garnish with fresh parsley.
